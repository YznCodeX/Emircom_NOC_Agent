## PROMPT (copy from here)

You are an experienced academic evaluator and AI engineering expert. I am a final-semester Artificial Intelligence student completing a COOP (cooperative education / internship) at Emircom, a Saudi telecom company. I am going to share my project documentation with you.

Please evaluate my project according to the **official UPM COOP rubric** described below, then give me:
1. A score for each rubric criterion
2. Specific, actionable feedback on how to improve each weak area
3. An overall assessment of the project's technical quality and presentation readiness
4. Questions you think the evaluation committee might ask me, and suggested answers

---

### OFFICIAL EVALUATION RUBRIC

The final grade has three components:

---

#### COMPONENT 1 — Presentation (40% of final grade)
Scored by an academic committee. Sub-categories:

**A. Content (5 items, each 1–5 scale, total ×5 = 25 points)**
1. Presented accurate information with clarity (deductions for typos)
2. Explained the departments at the training organization
3. Explained job titles, job descriptions, and tasks related to the trainee's major
4. Linked the training to at least two courses in the AI program
5. Mentioned and demonstrated mastery of at least one new skill

**B. Organization (4 items, each 1–5, total ×5 = 20 points)**
1. Appropriate introduction: self-introduction + organization intro + thesis statement + catches attention + explains flow
2. Smooth transitions and logical flow between topics
3. Clear documentation and explanation of ideas with acceptable-quality visuals
4. Closing that includes a summary and acknowledgement

**C. Professionalism (2 items, each 1–5, total ×5 = 10 points)**
1. Meeting deadline and punctuality
2. Professional appearance and self-confidence

**D. Delivery (4 items, each 1–5, total ×5 = 20 points)**
1. Eye contact and body language
2. Engaging the audience
3. Appropriate use of language
4. Appropriate use of technical presentation tools (PowerPoint, pointers, etc.)

**E. Response to Questions: 20 points (separate from above)**

---

#### COMPONENT 2 — Written Report (30% of final grade)
Scored item by item with penalties for missing elements:

| Section | Max Marks |
|---------|-----------|
| Cover Page | 3% |
| Abstract | 3% |
| Table of Contents | 3% |
| Introduction | 6% |
| Body | 12% |
| Conclusion | 3% |
| References (penalty for missing citations) | −penalty |
| Appendices (forms) | −penalty |
| **Total** | **30%** |

**Introduction must include:**
- Description of training site
- Clear thesis statement
- Objectives of the report
- Related theoretical courses
- At least one new skill learned
- Background information

**Body must include:**
- Sections and subsections in logical order
- Pictures from the training site with captions
- All figures and tables numbered and referenced in text

**Conclusion must include:**
- Summary of training experience (half to one page)
- Challenges encountered
- Suggestions for improvement

**Formatting rules (each violation = −1%, max −5%):**
- Font: Times New Roman or Arial
- Level 1 headings: Roman numerals (I, II, III), 16pt Bold
- Level 2 headings: Uppercase letters, 14pt Bold
- Level 3 headings: A.1, A.2 format, 14pt Normal
- Body text: 11pt, normal, single-line
- Moderate margins

---

#### COMPONENT 3 — Supervisor Evaluation (30% of final grade)
Scored by the site supervisor at Emircom:

**Team Work (4 items × 5 = 20 points)**
1. Listens to instructions, completes tasks on time
2. Coordinates tasks effectively with team members
3. Punctual attendance at meetings
4. Communicates effectively with supervisors and stakeholders

**Training Experience / Knowledge (2 items × 5 = 10 points)**
1. Applies conceptual knowledge from AI major to assigned tasks with minimal supervision
2. Designs experiments, observes measurements, analyzes and interprets data

---

### WHAT TO EVALUATE FROM THE DOCUMENTATION

The project documentation I am about to share represents:
- The **technical work** I built (what would be described in the Report Body and demonstrated in the Presentation)
- The **context and background** (what would appear in the Introduction)
- The **challenges and limitations** (what would appear in the Conclusion)
- The **objectives and courses linked** (for both Report and Presentation Content criteria)
- The **skills I learned** (for Presentation Content criterion 5 and Report Introduction)

---

### WHAT I WANT FROM YOU

Please structure your response as follows:

**SECTION 1 — Presentation Readiness Assessment**
For each of the 5 Content criteria, 4 Organization criteria, and the delivery/response areas: rate the project documentation's readiness (Poor / Fair / Average / Good / Excellent) and explain WHY. Then give ONE specific thing I could add or improve for each criterion.

**SECTION 2 — Report Quality Assessment**
For each report section (Cover, Abstract, ToC, Introduction, Body, Conclusion): tell me what is present, what is missing, and what would cost me marks. Score each section out of its maximum.

**SECTION 3 — Technical Quality Assessment**
Evaluate the project on:
- Complexity and ambition for a COOP project
- Appropriateness of the AI techniques used (LLMs, LangGraph, RAG, HITL)
- Quality of system design and architecture
- Honesty about limitations
- Readiness for a real production environment

**SECTION 4 — Likely Examination Questions**
Give me 8–10 questions the evaluation committee is likely to ask, and suggest strong answers for each.

**SECTION 5 — Priority Improvements**
List the 5 most important things I should fix or add before my presentation/submission, ranked by impact on my grade.

**SECTION 6 — Overall Estimated Grade**
Give an honest estimated score for each component (Presentation Content, Report, Technical Quality) and an overall assessment. Be critical — I need an honest evaluation, not encouragement.
